Payment Categories that can be made through the TelePago Popular® Service:

1. Automatic Payments – These payments allow you to preauthorize the Bank to make periodic Payments without having to give additional instructions each time you want the Bank to make a Payment. Automatic Payments can be:

  1. Fixed. You can authorize the Bank to debit from the Designated Account a fixed amount for a set period of time. ON/OFF service in Mi Banco Móvil

The ON / OFF feature allows Mi Banco Móvil users the option to use the OFF button to temporarily block the processing of certain debit card transactions. It also allows them to use the ON button to unlock a previously blocked card. The feature is available to account owners, signatories, and custodians only through the Mi Banco Móvil app.

If you choose to block a debit card, the following transactions and/or actions related to the blocked card will not be authorized by Banco Popular:

  • Purchases over the phone or online using the blocked card number
  • Card transactions present at the store or point of sale
  • Cash withdrawals at ATMs or points of sale
  • Enrollment or use of digital wallets (e.g. Samsung Pay or Google Pay)
  • Add the blocked card to the ATH Móvil® service
  • Complete the registration to Mi Banco using the blocked card as a card tied to the registration

Even if you block your debit card, Banco Popular will continue to process the following transactions or account activities:

  • Payments and transfers through TelePago Popular® and/or Mi Banco
  • Sending or receiving money through the Mobile Withdrawal and/or ATH Móvil® services
  • Deposits via Mobile Easy Deposit
  • Payments through ATH Móvil®
  • Regain access to Mi Banco
  • Recurring payments, such as subscriptions or monthly payments. It is the user's responsibility to verify with the merchant if the transaction is encoded as recurring
  • Actions taken before you opted to block the card, i.e. those that are delayed in their authorization (such as transactions in process)
  • Fees related to the service of your account such as monthly service

In the case of a card renewal or replacement of an existing card that you have previously blocked using the "OFF" functionality, you must proceed to the initial activation of the new card as you would normally do, but the existing card will continue to be "OFF" until you mark it "ON" in Mi Banco Móvil.
Any of the owners, signatories, and custodians of an active account in Mi Banco may use the ON/ OFF service to block and/or unlock any of the cards related to that account, even if the card that is blocked is the card used by someone else on the account. Banco Popular sends a notification to the owner, signatory or custodian who blocked or unblocked the card.
